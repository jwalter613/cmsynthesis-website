What it does

Built with intent.

Every feature on CMS Alpine exists because a musician needed it. Nothing is there for show.

01

Engineered for Writing

16-step sequencer, 8 tracks, parameter locks, and a 128×64 OLED that stays readable at arm's length in a dark studio. The workflow is direct: press a pad, hear a sound, build a pattern. No menu diving.

02

Built to Last

PA6-GF glass-filled nylon body. Black anodized aluminum knobs. Dark walnut riser. Black oxide screws throughout. When everything else on your desk looks tired in five years, this won't. And if something breaks, you can fix it.

03

Fully Open

Open firmware and open hardware. The source code, schematics, and STL files for every printed part will be on GitHub. Modify it, print your own case, fork the firmware. You own what you build.
